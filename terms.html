12. Cookies

A cookie is a term for the condition that a user's behavior on a network is registered with the user himself – Thus on the user's own computer. The advantage of this registration is that next time the user visits the same site, the server already knows who the visitor is. No personal identifiable information is stored in a cookie but only information about the user's behavior on the site, such as an entered username for login. A cookie is stored on the user's hard disk together with cached files. A cookie is thus a text file that is sent to your browser from a web server and stored on your computer's hard drive. You can set your browser to inform you when you receive a cookie or you can choose to turn cookies off completely. All these cookies are used to recognize your PC. Technically, cookies can be divided into two types. One type is called "session cookie" which is used to keep track of what you have added to your shopping cart, for example, while navigating around the site. These "session cookies" are NOT stored on your computer but disappear when you close your web browser. The other type of cookie is called "permanent cookie" and it is saved as a text file on your computer for approx. one month. A "permanent cookie" means that our server can recognize your computer the next time you log in to our website. Cheetah Shoes uses cookies to optimize the site and it’s functionalities to make the user experience as good and smooth as possible. You always have the option to delete cookies from your computer. For example in Internet Explorer, cookies are deleted under the "Tools" menu -> "Internet Options" -> "Delete Cookies." Log Statistics are used on Cheetah Shoes and means that a statistics system collects information that can provide a statistical view of how many visitors Our website has had where they come from, on which part of the site this was left etc. On Cheetah Shoes, log statistics are used to optimize the site and it’s functionality. Log statistics are thus used to enhance user-friendliness. Have fun with Cheetah Shoes.
